# Introduction to Fault Tolerance

## Why Should You Care About Fault Tolerance?

In today’s digital world, we expect our apps, websites, and systems to work smoothly at all times. But as technology becomes more complex and interconnected, things can (and will) go wrong. Fault tolerance is all about making sure that when something breaks, your system can handle it without skipping a beat. This introduction will give you a friendly overview of what fault tolerance is and why it matters.
