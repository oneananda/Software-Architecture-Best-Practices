# Disaster recovery (DR)-Best Practices

`Disaster Recovery (DR)` is a process companies use to get back on their feet after a disaster, whether it's a natural event (like a flood or earthquake), a system failure (like a server crash), or a cyberattack (like ransomware). It’s all about making sure that the business can quickly recover important systems and data to keep running smoothly, even after something goes wrong.


### Disaster Recovery Plan

A Sample DR plan will contains

- Overview of the plan
- Roles and responsibilities
- Risk assessment
- Recovery procedures
- Communication plan
- Recovery objectives
- Backup strategy
